COMMON DISEASE

Cold And Flu

Symptoms

  • fever
  • headache
  • more intense pain and fatigue
  • dry cough

Treatment

  • more and more rest.
  • drink lots of fluids.
  • stay away from cigrette smoke.

Allergies

Symptoms

  • eye irritation
  • runny nose
  • hives/skin rashes
  • itchiness

Treatment

  • antihistamines
  • decongestants
  • anti-inflammatory agents
  • allergy shots

Diarrhea

Symptoms

  • abdominal Cramps
  • bloating
  • nausea
  • vomiting
  • fever

Treatment

  • stay hydrated
  • maintain good hygiene
  • Try probiotics
  • rest
  • Seek medical attention

Stomach Aches

Symptoms

  • pain or discomfort
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • bloating and gas
  • constipation
  • loss of apetite

Treatment

  • bowel rest
  • hydration
  • heat therapy
  • home remedies
  • exercise

Mononucleosis

Symptoms

  • fatigue
  • sore throat
  • fever
  • swollen lymph nodes
  • headache

Treatment

  • rest
  • over-the-counter pain relievers such as acetaminophen (Tylenol) or ibuprofen (Advil, Motrin) can help relieve fever, sore throat, and muscle and joint pain
  • hydration
  • corticosteroids
  • antiviral medication

Chickenpox

Symptoms

  • rash
  • fever
  • fatigue
  • headache
  • loss of apetite

Treatment

  • Antihistamines
  • Acetaminophen or ibuprofen
  • cool baths
  • calamine lotion
  • antiviral medications

Malaria

Symptoms

  • fever
  • chills
  • headache
  • muscle and joint pain
  • headache

Treatment

  • antimalarial drugs
  • supportive care
  • hospitalization
  • prevention of complications such as anemia, cerebral malaria, etc.

Tetanus

Symptoms

  • stiffness in jaw muscles
  • stiffness in the neck and back muscles
  • spasms and rigidity in other muscles
  • high blood pressure and rapid heart rate
  • fever and sweating

Treatment

  • antitoxin
  • antibiotics
  • wound care
  • tetanus vaccine
  • supportive therapy

Tuberculosis

Symptoms

  • cough
  • fever
  • fatigue
  • loss of appetite
  • chest pain

Treatment

  • antibiotics
  • directly observed therapy (DOT)
  • supportive care
  • surgery
