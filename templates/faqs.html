Overview

What can i expect to find here ?

In a word: support. We understand that your health journey is just that, your journey. So we’ve created a space where you can find the support and resources that you need. From group conversations to one-to-one messaging to medically-reviewed articles, there’s something for everyone. Here’s just a taste of what you can expect to find:

  • Learn something new every day with community-led, text-based live chats.
  • Gain advice from those who have been there before with one-to-one messaging.
  • Discover curated medical resources from a library of content.
  • A Guide that can help you through it all. You can quickly identify the Guide by the badge next to her username.

New Member Basics

I'm new here. Where do i start ?

Welcome to the community! We’re so happy to have you here. We recommend a few things to help you get started:

  • Make sure that you’ve filled out your profile completely. Not only will this allow other members to get to know you, but it will also help us to curate the articles, posts, and members that best fit you and your journey.
  • Say “hello” and introduce yourself in our welcome forum! Posting in this forum is a great way to find other new members and get to know your community. You can find this forum at the top of the list—it’s called, Come say “hello!”
  • Enable your push notifications to receive the latest news updates, post mentions, and live chat alerts. You can edit your notification preferences from your account settings (Note: Tap on the gear icon at the top of your profile screen to edit).
  • Finally, join us for a live chat and get a feel for what’s happening in the community. These take place every Sunday through Thursday at 5:00 pm PT/8:00 pm ET in the Live Discussions group.
